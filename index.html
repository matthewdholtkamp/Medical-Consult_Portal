⚠️ Decision-Support Tool — Read Before Use

This site generates consult drafts and provides clinical decision support. It does not replace your clinical judgment, licensure, or medical decision-making. Use only de-identified information here.

This platform is NOT HIPAA compliant. Do NOT enter Protected Health Information (PHI).

Do not enter any of the following 18 direct identifiers (HIPAA Safe Harbor):

  1. Names (e.g., full name, initials).
  2. All geographic subdivisions smaller than a state (street address, city, county, ZIP code).
  3. All elements of dates (except year) directly related to an individual (birthdate, admission/discharge dates, death date); and ages >89.
  4. Telephone numbers.
  5. Fax numbers.
  6. Email addresses.
  7. Social Security numbers.
  8. Medical record numbers.
  9. Health plan beneficiary numbers.
  10. Account numbers.
  11. Certificate or license numbers.
  12. Vehicle identifiers and serial numbers, including license plates.
  13. Device identifiers and serial numbers.
  14. Web URLs.
  15. IP addresses.
  16. Biometric identifiers (e.g., fingerprints, voiceprints).
  17. Full-face photographs and comparable images.
  18. Any other unique identifying number, characteristic, or code.

Additional note — other information that may identify a person:

  • Unique combinations of demographics or clinical details (rare diagnoses, exact time/location of a rare event) that would allow someone with local knowledge to identify the patient.
  • Occupation with identifiable role/location (e.g., "the hospital CEO" at a small facility), specific incident descriptions, or location/time combos that narrow identity.
  • Free-text notes containing household details, small-community ties, or quoted speech that could be linked to an individual.

Actual-knowledge rule: Even if the 18 identifiers above are removed, do not enter any detail you know — from your own knowledge or context — could reasonably identify the individual.

By continuing you acknowledge this tool is for support only and not HIPAA compliant.
